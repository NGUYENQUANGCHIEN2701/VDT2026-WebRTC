## Phase Goal

**As a** participant in an active call, **I want to** mute my mic, turn my camera off, and see the other person's mute/camera status, call duration, and a self-view — **so that** the in-call experience matches a real video-call product.

<objective>
In-call experience vertical slice. With the lifecycle live (Plans 03-04), this plan adds the controls and indicators that make an active call usable: track.enabled mute/cam toggles with peer relay, remote mute/cam-off indicators, the duration timer counting from 'connected', the PiP self-view with cam-off avatar, and EC/NS defaults. After this plan, two connected users can mute, toggle camera, and see each other's media state and call duration.

Purpose: MEDIA-01 and MEDIA-06. Mute/cam is explicitly outside the lifecycle (D-14) — relay only, no Redis. This slice is independent of the recovery slice (Plan 06).

Output: mediaControls.ts + CallButtons additions + 3 new in-call components + media.ts EC/NS constraints + CallPage wiring + callActions media-state-relay handler. mediaControls.test.ts (Plan 01) goes GREEN.
</objective>
