    7. Hangup (CALL-07): during an active call either side clicks hang up → both see "Cuộc gọi kết thúc" with the duration.

    11. Refresh within grace (STAB-02): during an active call, refresh one tab → within ~15s the call rejoins (brief black ~1-2s) and continues; do NOT refresh past grace.
