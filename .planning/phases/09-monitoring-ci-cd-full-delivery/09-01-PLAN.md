<objective>
Replace the existing hand-rolled `AtomicLong`-based `CallMetrics` with real Micrometer `Counter`/`Gauge` beans registered in the Spring `MeterRegistry`, tagged by `instance` (per backend replica) and `call_type`/`end_reason` (per D-03/D-04), and exposed at `/actuator/prometheus`. This is the sole backend-side change needed for INFR-04 — Prometheus/Grafana in Plan 09-02 depend on these exact metric names existing and being correct.

Purpose: Today's `CallMetrics` counters are invisible to Prometheus (never registered with a `MeterRegistry`) and are actively wrong for a scrape-based monitoring model (cron-reset fights Prometheus's monotonic-counter + windowed-query semantics). Per D-03/D-04, the new metrics must count 1-1 AND group calls together (tagged, not two disjoint families) and the success-rate denominator must include all 6 end-reasons from the Phase 4 taxonomy.
Output: `micrometer-registry-prometheus` on the classpath; `/actuator/prometheus` exposing `vdt_ws_sessions_active`, `vdt_calls_active{call_type}`, `vdt_calls_ended_total{call_type,end_reason}`, each carrying a common `instance` tag.
</objective>
